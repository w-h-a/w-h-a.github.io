Resume Read Chimera Case Study

Chimera reduces the risk of introducing bugs that propagate through your microservice infrastructure by gradually shifting traffic to a canary version of your service while automating simple threshold canary analysis.

Chimera supports canary deployments for microservices that run on ECS/Fargate and communicate via App Mesh. A Prometheus-configured CloudWatch agent is used for monitoring metrics, which Chimera sets up before it performs canary deployments.
